The objective of this project is to provide consumers with Food & Color Additives allowed in the United States and the European Union. This model shows the difference of allowed Food & Color Additives between the two entities and seeks to focus and explore those differences.These parameter values were selected to address both the shortfalls of existing tools as well as to address the interests of my target audience:

A plethora of data were acquired and analyzed to create this model. Among those data sets are:

  • Food Additive list: Food & Drug Administration https://www.fda.gov/food/food-additives-petitions/food-additive-status-list
  • Food Additive list: European Comissionhttps://webgate.ec.europa.eu/foods_system/main/?sector=FAD&auth=SANCAS
